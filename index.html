Download Complete!

Follow these steps to install adobe Reader and open your PDF

1

Locate the Installer

Look at the top right corner of your browser for the download icon (it looks like a down arrow ⬇️). or, open your Downloads folder on your computer: This PC → Downloads.

2

Run the Installer

Double-click on the "adobeReaderInstaller" file to start the installation

3

Follow Installation Steps

Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the adobe Reader installation

4

Open Your PDF

Once installed, locate the PDF file you want to open and double-click it

5

Enjoy adobe Reader

You can now view, edit, and interact with your PDF documents
